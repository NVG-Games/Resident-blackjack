# Privacy Policy — Resident Blackjack

**Last updated:** March 27, 2026

## Overview

Resident Blackjack is a single-player and local multiplayer card game. It does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.

## Data Collection

We do not collect any data. Specifically:

- No personal information is collected (name, email, location, etc.)
- No usage analytics or telemetry are sent
- No crash reports are transmitted
- No advertising identifiers are used
- No third-party SDKs or trackers are included

## Data Storage

All game state (scores, progress) exists only in local device memory and is never sent anywhere.

## Third-Party Services

This app does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services.

## Children's Privacy

This app does not collect any data from anyone, including children under 13.
